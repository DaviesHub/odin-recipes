Tequila Chili

Description

This simple and quick one-pot chili has great flavor and is nice and meaty.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can italian-style stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tabespoon chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium heat
  2. Stir in the tomatoes, kidney beans, onion, garlic
  3. Add tomato sauce, cumin powder, cayenne pepper, chilli powder, tequila and salt
  4. SImmer for 20 minutes
