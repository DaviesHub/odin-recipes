Basic Crepes

Description

Delicious crepes can be made in minutes and can be dressed up with sweet or savory toppings and any number of flavorful fillings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose fluor
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Whisk fluor and eggs in large bowl.
  2. Gradually add milk and water, stir to combine. Add salt, butter and beat till smooth.
  3. Heat lightly oiled pan over medium heat. Scoop batter onto pan using about 1/4 cup for each crepe.
  4. Cook for about 2 minutes until bottom is light brown.
